About SkillMatch Pro

SkillMatch Pro is a smart project allocation system that helps organizations manage project assignments efficiently. It allows administrators to add both projects and employees into the system. Based on the required skills for each project and the individual skill sets of employees, the system automatically allocates the most suitable employees to the respective projects.

Employees can log in to view their assigned projects and update the status of their tasks. This streamlines project management, improves transparency, and ensures optimal utilization of talent across the organization.
